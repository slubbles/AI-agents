## Pitch Ammunition (from Brain KB)

### The Problem (use 1-2 in personalized line)
- **72% ghosting rate:** "72% of freelancers experienced client ghosting in 2024"
- **No platform transparency:** "No published project completion, abandonment, or delay rate benchmarks exist from major freelance platforms"
- **Zero completion data:** Upwork's 2025 Transparency Report doesn't disclose completion rates

### Your Differentiation
- **Fixed scope eliminates scope creep** — 62% of solo tech projects run over budget
- **Done in 5 days** — vs. hiring/onboarding cycle of weeks
- **Half upfront, half on approval** — they don't risk full payment
- **Next.js is your lane** — SprintPage, Superside, Flowout all do Webflow only

### Competitor Intel (if they mention alternatives)
- SprintPage: 48-hour Webflow, unlimited revisions — but Webflow only
- Superside: Enterprise pricing, CRO focus — but Webflow only
- Flowout: Monthly subscription model — but Webflow only
- **Your angle:** "Nobody productizes Next.js builds. Webflow limits customization."

> "I saw [COMPANY] sells [PRODUCT]. A properly structured Next.js landing page with schema markup would dramatically improve your Google Shopping visibility."

## Response Handling

**"Tell me more"** → Send detailed scope document (listing all deliverables)

**"Can you do it cheaper?"** → "The price reflects the fixed-scope guarantee. Cheaper freelance options exist but don't come with the same timeline or revision policy."

**"Do you have examples?"** → (After first sale, show that case study)

**"I already have applicants"** → "Happy to proceed if they fall through. Many hiring processes stall — this is standing by if you want a faster path."
